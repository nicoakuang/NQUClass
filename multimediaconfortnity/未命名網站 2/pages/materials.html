Here you will find the basic building blocks for learning Traditional Chinese.

1. Basic Traditional Characters (基本漢字)

Let's start with some fundamental characters. Pay attention to their shape, Pinyin (pronunciation), and meaning. We use Traditional Characters (繁體字).

Character (漢字) Pinyin (拼音) Meaning (意思)
一 yī One
人 rén Person / People
大 dà Big / Large
天 tiān Sky / Day
我 wǒ I / Me
你 nǐ You
好 hǎo Good / Well

2. Pinyin System (拼音系統)

Pinyin (拼音) is the official system to transcribe Chinese characters into the Roman alphabet. It's crucial for learning pronunciation. It consists of Initials (like 'b', 'p', 'm'), Finals (like 'a', 'o', 'an'), and Tones (which indicate pitch changes).

Example: 媽 (mā - mother - high tone), 麻 (má - hemp - rising tone), 馬 (mǎ - horse - falling-rising tone), 罵 (mà - scold - falling tone).

3. Basic Vocabulary (基本詞彙)

Greetings (問候語):

  • 你好 (nǐ hǎo) - Hello
  • 謝謝 (xièxie) - Thank you
  • 不客氣 (bú kèqi) - You're welcome
  • 再見 (zàijiàn) - Goodbye
  • 對不起 (duìbuqǐ) - Sorry
  • 沒關係 (méi guānxi) - It's okay / No problem

Numbers (數字):

  • 一 (yī) - One
  • 二 (èr) - Two
  • 三 (sān) - Three
  • 四 (sì) - Four
  • 五 (wǔ) - Five
  • 六 (liù) - Six
  • 七 (qī) - Seven
  • 八 (bā) - Eight
  • 九 (jiǔ) - Nine
  • 十 (shí) - Ten
