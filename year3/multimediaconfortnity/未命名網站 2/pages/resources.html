Learning Resources (學習資源)

Here are some useful online resources to help you on your journey to learn Traditional Chinese:

Online Dictionaries (線上詞典) 📖

  • MDBG Chinese Dictionary - A comprehensive online dictionary that supports Traditional characters.
  • Pleco - A must-have dictionary app for your smartphone (iOS/Android).
  • 萌典 (Moedict) - The R.O.C. (Taiwan) Ministry of Education's dictionary, excellent for Taiwanese Mandarin standards.

Learning Apps (學習應用程式) 📱

  • Skritter - Excellent for practicing character writing, including stroke order (supports Traditional).
  • Duolingo / Memrise - Popular apps, check their current support level for Traditional Chinese.

Other Websites & Channels (其他網站) 🌐

  • Search on YouTube for "Learn Traditional Chinese" or "學繁體中文" - Many channels offer free lessons.
  • Look for blogs and forums dedicated to learning Mandarin.
  • Glossika - Offers sentence-based learning (supports Taiwanese Mandarin).
