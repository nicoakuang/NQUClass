Welcome! Start Your Journey Here! (歡迎光臨! 從這裡開始您的旅程!)

This website is designed to help you take your first steps into the fascinating world of Traditional Chinese. Here’s how you can make the most of it:

How to Use This Website:

Step 1: Learn the Basics (學習基礎)

Head over to the Materials section. Here you'll find lessons on essential Traditional Characters (漢字), the Pinyin (拼音) pronunciation system, and basic vocabulary (詞彙).

Step 2: Test Your Knowledge (測試知識)

Ready to check what you've learned? Visit the Exercises page! We have interactive quizzes to help you practice and reinforce your understanding.

Step 3: Explore More (探索更多)

Need more tools? The Resources page contains links to useful online dictionaries, learning apps, and other helpful websites to support your learning.

We hope you enjoy your learning experience. 加油! (Jiāyóu!)
